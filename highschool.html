GOVERNMENT BILINGUAL HIGH SCHOOL KRIBI

This is the high school where I did most of my secondary school academic years.

It is located in Kribi II, just in front of the beach. Unfortunately, in the last 4 years it has recorded more than 15 student deaths, some of which were my friends and people I knew.

One of them died just last year along with three of his friends I had seen the day before. I was like "It is not possible". But even with all the challenges, my years in this school were overall fun and inspiring.
